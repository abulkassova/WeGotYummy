FAQ
How do I search for recipes ?
You can easily search for recipes by using the search bar at the top of the screen. Later, you can filter your results by cuisine, dietary restrictions, preparation time, and more.
How do I submit my own recipes?
To share your culinary creations with the community, simply create an account, log in, and navigate to your profile. From there, you can add and publish your recipes.
Can I print or save recipes ?
Yes, Recepies can be saved and downloaded for offline use. Look for the "Print" or "Save" options on each recipe page.
